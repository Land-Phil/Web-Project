Hello! I am a Supply Chain Management graduate from Auburn University, currently serving as an AOG Sourcing Specialist at Hawaiian Airlines in Technical Operations. My professional journey began in 2021 with several rotations in Parker Hannifin’s Aerospace Group as part of their Leadership Development Program. This experience led me from Camarillo to Kalamazoo, with a significant focus on honing my skills in procurement and demand planning.

Driven by a passion for continuous growth, I approach my career with an open mind. Over the last few years, I've dedicated myself to tailoring a skill set that seamlessly integrates the technical proficiency of statisticians and data scientists with the operational know-how of supply chain and manufacturing professionals.

In the dynamic landscape of the information age, I recognize that business acumen is increasingly tied to domains such as big data, analytics, and machine learning. I firmly believe that those with an ability to both see the big picture and “speak data” will drive the most impactful change. Embracing this interdisciplinary approach, I hope to be at the forefront of innovation in my field.
